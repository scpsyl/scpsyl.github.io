Intro

Yushan Liu received his B.S. degree in Electronic Information Science and Technology from Jilin University in 2024. Recently he is pursuing his M.S. degree in Data Science and Information Technology at Smart Sensing and Robotics group (SSR Group) at Tsinghua Shenzhen International School. He concentrated on the research topic of embodied AI under the guidance of Prof. Wenbo Ding and Prof. Xiao-Ping Zhang in SSR Group.
